About GasGo

GasGo is your trusted LPG (liquefied petroleum gas) delivery partner. We make it fast and easy to order safe, reliable cooking gas right to your doorstep for homes, hostels, and small businesses.

What we do

On‑demand LPG delivery

Place an order and our vetted riders deliver filled, sealed cylinders to you promptly. No queues, no transport hassle.

Safe cylinder exchange

We exchange your empty cylinder for a certified, leak‑tested one that matches your brand/valve type where available.

Emergency top‑ups

Run out unexpectedly? Use our emergency option for priority delivery during extended hours.
