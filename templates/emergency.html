Emergency Protocol.

If you suspect a gas leak, see flames, or smell gas strongly, prioritize your safety and follow these steps immediately.

Call Toll-Free: 0800 720 000 Fire Brigade: 999
01

Shut Valve

Turn off the cylinder valve ONLY if safe to do so.

02

No Sparks

Do NOT flip electrical switches or use open flames.

03

Ventilate

Open all windows and doors. Evacuate the premises.

04

Call Help

Call the emergency lines from a safe distance outside.

Safety Checklist

  • Keep cylinders upright and in well-ventilated areas.
  • Check hose pipes for cracks every 3 months.
  • Only use soapy water (not matches) to check for leaks.
